Frequently Asked Questions

What can I do after Commerce?

You can pursue B.Com, BBA, CA, CS, CMA, CFA, MBA and many other career paths.

Which is better: B.Com or BBA?

Both are valuable. B.Com is more finance and accounting focused, while BBA focuses on management.

Is CA difficult?

CA requires dedication and consistent study, but it is one of the most respected professional qualifications.

What is the highest salary career in Commerce?

Investment Banking, CA, CFA, Financial Analyst and Corporate Finance roles often offer high salary potential.

Can Commerce students get government jobs?

Yes. Banking, SSC, RBI, NABARD, Insurance and many government sectors recruit commerce graduates.

Which skills are important for Commerce students?

Accounting, GST, Excel, Power BI, Financial Analysis, Communication and Taxation are highly valuable skills.
